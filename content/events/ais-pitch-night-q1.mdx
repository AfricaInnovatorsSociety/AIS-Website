## What to expect

AIS Pitch Night is the heartbeat of our community — a no-fluff, fast-paced evening where eight CMU-Africa founders take the stage with **3 minutes** to convince a panel of judges and a room full of peers that their idea deserves to exist.

### The format

- 3-minute pitch, 4-minute Q&A
- Live judge scoring across **clarity, traction, team, and ambition**
- Audience choice award voted live by the room
- Networking mixer immediately after with light food and drinks

### Who should come

If you're building something, thinking about building something, or want to back the next generation of African founders — this is your night.
