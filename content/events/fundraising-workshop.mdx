## Inside the room

Most fundraising advice on the internet was written for Sand Hill Road. Ours is written for Kigali, Lagos, Nairobi, and Cape Town.

Join us for a 90-minute workshop with active African investors and operator-founders who've raised on the continent. Bring your deck and your hardest questions.

### Agenda

1. The African capital stack — angels, family offices, regional VCs, global funds
2. What investors actually look for at pre-seed and seed
3. Live deck teardowns (3 selected attendees)
4. Q&A and 1:1 office hours

### Prerequisites

Come having read your own deck out loud at least once. That's it.
