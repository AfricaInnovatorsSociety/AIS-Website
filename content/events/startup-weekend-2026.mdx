## 54 hours to build something real

Startup Weekend is the boldest event we run all year. You arrive Friday evening with an idea (or none at all), form a team within an hour, and have until Sunday evening to validate, build, and pitch.

### The weekend at a glance

- **Friday** — Pitch fire-round, team formation, dinner
- **Saturday** — Customer interviews, MVP build, mentor office hours
- **Sunday** — Final builds, dress-rehearsal, pitch finale, judging, awards

### Prizes

- Grand prize: incubation slot + cash grant
- Runner-up: mentorship package
- Audience choice: surprise

### What you'll need

- A laptop, an open mind, and the willingness to be wrong fast.
