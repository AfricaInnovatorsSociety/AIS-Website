## A new home for African founders

The Africa Innovators Society exists because the most promising entrepreneurs on the continent are often the ones already on campus, they just need the right room to walk into.

This year, we're committing to:

- **Weekly programming**, a workshop, a mentor session, or a pitch night every single week
- **Real money**, grants and prizes funded through our sponsor network
- **Real networks**, mentor matching, alumni access, and operator office hours
- **Real ventures**, at least 5 new ventures launched out of AIS by year-end

If you're a CMU-Africa student, joining is one form away. If you're a mentor, sponsor, or partner, we'd love to hear from you.
