## Read this before your next investor call

Most of the fundraising content you'll find online was written for founders in San Francisco. African capital is structurally different, and pretending otherwise wastes months you don't have.

### Start with operators, not VCs

The most useful first checks usually come from operators who've shipped in your sector. They write smaller cheques, ask sharper questions, and open doors VCs cannot.

### Get to revenue fast

In African markets, traction beats narrative. A $500/month subscription business beats a beautiful deck almost every time.

### Optimize for the next 6 months, not the next 6 years

Raise just enough to hit the next clearly defined milestone. Then raise again. Long runways feel safe and dull your edge.
